The who

My name is Vincent. I am a lifelong learner, dedicated to make the product and services that surround us more human-centered.

My approach of design is deeply shaped by my knowledge in Cognitive Sciences, and my eagerness to learn turns every new problem into a challenge that I must succeed.

The why

When you develop a product or a service, lot of skills are required. Engineers, Creative, Commercial... Above all, you need to listen to users. UX Designer are here to gather user data, behavior and expectations, and turn it into tangible, goal-oriented solutions to design and boost a product. From conversion to fidelity, we are here to make sure the product meets the company’s and user’s needs.

The how

I am a T-shaped profile. My curiosity makes me want to learn about every subjects, and by experience, I know on what to focus my energy to make the best out of a project. Designing the things that surrounds us, I value design that has a positive impact on society.
